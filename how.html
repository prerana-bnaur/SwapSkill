How It Works

Step 1: Create Your Skill Card

Sign up and fill out your profile. Highlight what you're good at and what you'd like to learn. Your Skill Card becomes your currency.

Step 2: Match & Connect

Our system finds you the most compatible swap partners based on your preferences. You can browse, message, and set up sessions directly.

Step 3: Skill Exchange

Teach in your own way — through calls, notes, mini projects or live sessions — and learn just as personally. No pressure, just progress.
